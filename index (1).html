About Us

Built on discipline, brotherhood, and professional excellence.

FAREAST LEGION SF PARATROOPERS is composed of airborne personnel, Special Forces, elite police forces, military members, bureaus, and federal agency members from active, reserve, call active duty, and retired backgrounds.

The organization is built on shared experience, service, discipline, brotherhood, and commitment to mission-oriented standards across allied countries and professional communities.
