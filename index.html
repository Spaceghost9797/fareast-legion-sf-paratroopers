Who We Are

FAREAST LEGION SF PARATROOPERS is an association composed of paratroopers, Special Forces, elite police, military personnel, bureaus, and allied-country counterparts, including active duty personnel, reserve personnel, and retired personnel.


The organization is built on shared experience, discipline, service, brotherhood, international camaraderie, and a strong commitment to professional excellence.

Mission

To preserve airborne heritage, strengthen brotherhood, promote discipline, and uphold professional excellence among elite forces, law enforcement personnel, military members, allied-country counterparts, and qualified individuals.

Vision

To become a respected international association built on service, tactical readiness, leadership, airborne heritage, international camaraderie, and enduring legacy.

Awards and Certification Notice

All badges, certificates, and qualification awards shall be issued only by the authorized host or instructor who conducted the respective program.


However, the issuance, recognition, classification, or modification of such awards shall remain subject to the approval of FLP, based on qualifications deemed fit and appropriate. Certain badges, certificates, or qualification awards may not be applicable to all programs and shall be issued only when relevant to the nature, scope, and requirements of the respective program conducted.

Membership

Membership is intended for qualified paratroopers, Special Forces, elite police, military personnel, bureaus, allied-country counterparts, active duty personnel, reserve personnel, retired personnel, and qualified individuals who share the values of discipline, service, brotherhood, international camaraderie, and professional excellence.
